PhysioCare Portal Business Overview · §1

1. Executive Summary

PhysioCare Portal is an internal clinic management system designed for physiotherapy practices operating one or more clinic locations, with optional home-visit care.

It replaces fragmented spreadsheets and paper records with a single workspace for patient records, therapy scheduling, session completion, revenue collection, staff management, and operational cost tracking — all in Indian Rupees (₹).

🎯

Primary Goal

Give clinic admins and physiotherapists one calm workspace to manage patients, sessions, and finances without switching tools.

🏢

Multi-Clinic Ready

Support multiple clinic locations with staff scoped to their assigned clinic, while admins see everything.

🏠

Home & Clinic Care

Separate workflows for in-clinic therapy and home-only patients, each with dedicated dashboards.

📊

Revenue Visibility

Track actual collected revenue vs estimated revenue from scheduled session amounts.

5. Scheduling & Sessions

Session Types

TypeWhereTime SlotsScheduling From
ClinicAt clinic location09:00 – 20:30 (30-min slots)Sessions page, Calendar, Patient profile, Bulk scheduler

Therapy Configuration

  • Therapy types: US, TENS, IFT, SWD, Wax Bath, Cupping, Dry Needling, Exercise, Manual Therapy, and more (multi-select)
  • Levels: Basic, Rehab, Advance — shown as colour-coded badges
  • Dual therapy: Schedule two therapy types at different times on the same day
  • Bulk scheduling: Create many sessions at once by count + frequency, or date range + frequency

Session Lifecycle

ScheduledSession booked; optional estimated amount (₹)
→
CompletedTreatment notes + amount collected recorded

Other statuses: Cancelled No show

Calendar Quick-Booking

From the Calendar day view, click any empty slot to book instantly. Supports booking multiple patients into the same time slot (group sessions). Each patient gets their own session record.

Walk-In Recording

From the Sessions page, staff can record a completed walk-in session without prior scheduling — useful for same-day arrivals.

PhysioCare Portal Business Overview · §6

6. Home Visit Operations

Home visit workflows are intentionally separated from clinic operations so field teams can focus on home-care patients without clinic noise.

Home Dashboard

  • Total home patients and visit status breakdown (scheduled, completed, cancelled)
  • Upcoming home visits list with quick navigation to patient profiles
  • "Needs attention" — patients with no upcoming or no completed visits

Home Visits Page

  • Schedule bulk home visits: select patient, therapy type, level, start date, time slot, number of visits, repeat interval
  • Patient cards collapsed by default — expand to see scheduled visits with actions
  • Mark visits complete with treatment notes and amount collected

Patient Profile — Home Visit Log

Home-only patients have an interactive calendar on their profile. Staff mark individual days as done, which automatically creates or updates the linked therapy session and syncs revenue data.

7. Revenue Tracking

All amounts are in Indian Rupees (₹). The portal distinguishes between money already collected and money expected from upcoming sessions.

MetricWhen it countsWhere shown
Actual revenue Collected Session status = Completed AND amount collected is entered Dashboard revenue cards, Sessions totals, Patient "Total paid"
Estimated revenue Est. Session status = Scheduled AND amount was pre-set at booking time Dashboard, Sessions page, scheduled session badges ("Est. ₹…")

PhysioCare Portal Business Overview · §8–9

8. Reports & Documents

Patient reports (X-rays, prescriptions, assessment PDFs) can be attached to any patient record during add or edit.

  • Each report has a title, date, optional notes, and optional file upload
  • Supported formats: PDF, JPEG, PNG, WebP, DOC, DOCX (max 10 MB)
  • Files stored securely in Cloudflare R2 cloud storage when configured
  • Reports viewable from the patient profile; deleted automatically when patient is removed

9. Administration

Clinics (Admin)

Create and manage clinic locations. Assign staff members to clinics. Activate or deactivate clinics without deleting history.

Staff (Admin)

Add staff with job title (Consultant Physiotherapist, Assistant, Intern, or custom), role (Admin/Staff), clinic assignment, and login credentials. Approve pending registrations.

PhysioCare Portal Business Overview · §10

10. Key User Journeys

Journey A — New clinic patient, first session

  1. Admin/staff logs in → Patients → Add patient → Clinic patient
  2. Fill personal + clinical details → Save
  3. Open patient profile → Schedule clinic → Choose therapy types, dates, optional amount
  4. On session day → Sessions page → Mark complete → Enter notes + amount collected
  5. Revenue appears on Clinic Dashboard as actual collected revenue

Journey B — Home-only patient care

  1. Add patient → Home only → Fill caregiver + condition details
  2. Home Visits → Schedule bulk visits (e.g. 12 visits, every 2 days)
  3. Track on Home Dashboard; expand patient card to manage individual visits
  4. Mark days complete on profile calendar or Home Visits list

Journey C — Admin monthly review

  1. Clinic Dashboard → Review revenue, session volume, upcoming appointments
  2. Expenses & Equipment → Check monthly spend and inventory condition
  3. Staff → Approve any pending registrations
  4. Calendar → Review clinic capacity and booking patterns
Demo mode

Without cloud configuration, the portal runs in demo mode using browser local storage. All features work locally for evaluation; connect Supabase and R2 for production persistence.
